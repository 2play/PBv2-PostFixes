The XEGS release was backed by new games, including 
Barnyard Blaster and Bug Hunt,
plus cartridge ports of older games, such as 
Fight Night (Accolade, 1985),
Lode Runner (Broderbund, 1983),
Necromancer (Synapse Software, 1982),
Ballblazer (Lucasfilm Games, 1985).
Support for the system was dropped in 1992 along with rest of Atari's 8-bit computers as well as the Atari 2600 and Atari 7800.
